Light Sources


Light Sources are a standard concept in art and design as nothing can look real if their shadows are wrong or even nonexsistent! Therefore, this art is one of the most important and crucial. The lack of colors was due to it being my introductory assignment and to make it easier, it was only a gray scale.
Color and Hue


The next step was introducing color and hue into the equation. This art piece allowed me to understand how color and backlighting can influence the overall theme of the piece.
Colored Light Source
